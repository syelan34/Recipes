## Ingredients
###### Serves 2
1/2 lbs [[Carne Asada Marinade]]
1 cup [[Salsa Verde]]
1/4 cup heavy cream
Diced red onion
Cilantro
Queso fresco
Diced tomato or tomatillos
Sliced lettuce
1 avocado
Tortilla chips
## Directions
Grill steak on high heat for 5-7 minutes on each side.
Remove from grill, cover in aluminum foil and let rest for 10 minutes.
Heat salsa verde and heavy cream in a large skillet on medium-high heat.
Add chips, gently turning to coat.
Reduce heat, cover, and let simmer 2 minutes.
Top with steak and sprinkle with toppings.

Serve with additional salsa verde and additional toppings.
