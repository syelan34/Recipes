## Ingredients
2 bags fresh cranberries (handful reserved)
Allspice berries
Star anise
Cloves
Cinnamon sticks
1 large orange
1 1/3 cup sugar
## Directions
Make a sachet to hold the star anise, allspice, cloves, and cinnamon sticks.
Zest and juice orange, mix water with juice totalling 2 cups.
In a mixing bowl combine sugar, orange zest, cranberries, sachet, and juice mixture.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered until cranberries burst.
Remove lid and simmer until reduced.
Remove sachet, add reserved cranberries.
