## Ingredients
3 cups rolled oats
1 cup flour
1/2 cup flax seed
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 cups grape seed oil
3/4 cups maple syrup
## Directions
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Combine oats, flour, flax seed, salt, and cinnamon, mixing well.
Whisk together oil and syrup.
Pour wet ingredients over dry and stir to combine.
Spread evenly on baking sheet covered by parchment paper.
Bake 30 minutes or until golden brown.
Let sit for 5 minutes then cut into bars.
