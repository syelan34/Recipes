# Csipetke

### Ingredients
~1kg beef (sometimes I use pork)
~1kg potatoes 
1 large onion
1 bell pepper
1 tomato 
4 cloves of garlic
3 tbsp of paprika (I prefer hot paprika)
1 tbsp salt
### Directions
1. Dice potatoes and beef into cubes
2. Dice the onion, tomato, and bell pepper
3. Roughly mince the garlic
4. Preheat a pot or dutch oven on medium-high heat, then brown the beef with some oil
5. Turn heat to medium-low, then add the onions and garlic, stir until fragrant
6. Add the paprika and stir until the oil is stained red
7. Add the diced bell pepper, tomato, salt, and enough water to cover the beef
8. Bring the heat back to medium-high until the water starts to bubble, then change it to low and cover the pot, allowing to simmer for an hour
9. Add potatoes and simmer for another hour
10. Taste broth and add more seasoning as you see fit
