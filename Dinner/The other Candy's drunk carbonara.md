## Ingredients
1 box pasta (used small shells)
1 package thin/american style bacon (sliced vaguely into squares)
1 can green beans
Worcestershire sauce
Gochujang paste
Onion powder
Garlic powder
Salt
Shitty sprinkle parmesan
## Directions
Boil water for pasta while you fry the bacon bits with however much gochujang.
Cook-a da pasta (bacon might take a while)
Drain beans and add to bacon
When pasta is cooked to preference, save some of the starch water and drain the rest.
Recombine everything in the pasta pot and toss it around for a bit till it gets thicc
