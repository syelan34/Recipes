## Ingredients
2 racks of pork back ribs
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp mustard powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
Salt & pepper
BBQ sauce
## Directions
Mix herbs etc + make the rub
Apply to meaty side of ribs
Refrigerate 24-72 hours wrapped in aluminum foil
Bake 3 hours at 275°F still wrapped in foil
Cover in BBQ sauce and heat on BBQ for 10 minutes to caramelize
