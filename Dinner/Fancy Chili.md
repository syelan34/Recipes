## Ingredients
4 slices bacon
1 large onion, diced
1 garlic clove, minced
1.5 lbs lean ground beef
1 can crushed tomatoes
1 can tomato sauce
1 cup beer
1 can black beans
1.5 tbsp chili powder
1.5 tsp cumin
1.5 tsp paprika
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp cayenne powder
## Directions
In a large, heavy bottomed pot, cook bacon slices until browned
Add onion and garlic, cook until translucent
Add ground beef and cook until browned
Empty most of the fat (leave small amount)
Stir in the rest of the ingredients
Bring to a boil
Reduce heat and let simmer at least 30 minutes.
