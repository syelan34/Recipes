## Ingredients
2 lbs ground lean beef or lamb
2 cans cream corn
6 potatoes
1 onion
1 clove garlic
Chives
Thyme
Butter
Paprika
Salt
Pepper
## Directions
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Cube potatoes and boil until soft. 
Mash while adding butter until creamy but still firm, adding salt & pepper to taste.
Dice onions and sauté in olive oil until clear.
Add minced garlic and thyme.
Add ground beef or lamb and stir until browned.
Add meat to baking dish and cover with cream corn.
Add layer of mashed potatoes, sprinkling paprika and minced chives to taste.
Use fork to score mashed potatoes or otherwise decorate top. (optional)
Bake 30 min or until top crust turns golden.
