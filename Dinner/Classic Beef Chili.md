## Ingredients
###### Serves 8
1 onion, finely chopped
1 jalapeño, finely chopped
2 tbsp butter
2 lbs ground beef
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1 can 28oz diced tomatoes
1 can 19oz kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup regular or strong coffee
1/4 cup beef broth or water
1/4 cup ketchup
3 tbsp lime juice
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
## Directions
In a large saucepan, brown the onion and pepper in the butter.
Add the meat and brown thoroughly.
Season with salt and pepper.
Add the garlic and spices and cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
Add the remaining ingredients except the cilantro, and bring to a boil. 
Simmer half-covered for 45 minutes until the chili thickens. 
Add the cilantro.
Adjust seasoning to taste.
