## Ingredients
###### Serves 4
1 shallot, diced
1 1/2 tbsp ginger
1 tbsp berbéré spice
2 lbs stewing beef
1 cup beef stock
2 boiled eggs, quartered
6 tbsp butter
## Directions
In large pot, melt 2 tbsp butter and fry shallot for 15 min.
Add 2 tbsp butter and ginger, cook for 10 min.
Add 2 tbsp butter and berbéré spice, cook for 5 min.
Add stewing beef and beef stock, simmer for 2-3 min.
Add eggs and mix.

Serve over rice or with bread.
