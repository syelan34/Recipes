## Ingredients
1 box elbow macaroni or cavatappi
2 tbsp butter
1 jalapeño
1 large onion
1 green bell pepper
2 large eggs
5 cups fresh grated sharp cheddar (1 cup reserved)
Bread crumbs
2 cans evaporated milk
Paprika
Salt
Pepper
### Directions
Boil pasta al dente.
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Finely chop jalapeño (remove seeds if less spice preferred), onion and bell pepper.
Sauté veggies in butter until soft.
Add eggs to cool condensed milk and whisk together.
Pour milk-eggs mixture into sautéed veggies.
Add 4 cups of cheese, stir until melted.
Season sauce to taste with paprika, salt & pepper.
Add macaroni to sauce.
Spoon mac and cheese into baking dish, cover with aluminum foil..
Bake for 20 minutes at 350°F.
Remove foil, sprinkle reserved cheese and breadcrumbs.
Broil on high until golden brown (5-10 min).
