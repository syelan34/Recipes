## Ingredients
##### Marinade:
1 lime, zested
6 tbsp olive oil
3 tbsp maple syrup
1 tbsp coriander seeds
2 tsp cumin powder

##### Tray:
12 chicken drumsticks
1 cauliflower, cut into cubes
1 cup seedless red grapes
## Directions
Make 3 slices on each drumstick
Place drumsticks in half the marinade
Place cauliflower and grapes in other half of marinate
Soak overnight
Preheat oven to 425°F
On a tray with parchment paper, bake drumsticks for 25 minutes
Add cauliflower and grapes and bake another 25 minutes
