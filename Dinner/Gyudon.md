## Ingredients
###### Serves 4
1 onion, sliced into strips
1 green onion, thinly sliced diagonally
1 lbs lean beef, sliced thinly (approximately 1/4" strips)
1 cup dashi
4 tbsp sake (substitute with sherry, chinese rice wine or water)
4 tbsp mirin
6 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp sugar
4 servings cooked Japanese short-grain rice
## Directions
In a large, room temperature frying pan add dashi, sake, mirin, soy sauce and sugar.
Stir until sugar dissolved.
Separate onion slices and spread throughout broth.
Layer beef over top of onions, making sure to cover the onions.
Cover pan with lid, and heat to medium.
Once simmering, turn down heat to simmer and let cooked 3-4 minutes.
While simmering, skim off fat with a fine-mesh skimmer.
Sprinkle green onions on top and let cook for another minute.

Serve over rice, and drizzle some of the sauce over the rice.
