## Ingredients
##### Marinade:
3 tbsp olive oil
3 tbsp honey
2 tbsp sriracha sauce
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp fennel seeds

##### Tray:
12 chicken thighs, boneless
1 large sweet potato, cubed
1 fennel bulb, sliced
8 medjool dates
### Directions
Prepare marinade
Marinate all ingredients overnight, except dates
Preheat oven to 425°F
Bake all ingredients except dates on tray with parchment for 25 minutes
Add dates, bake for 10 more minutes
