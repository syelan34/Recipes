## Ingredients

1/2 cup light mayo
2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 tsp sugar
Coarse salt + pepper
1/2 head cabbage
2 medium carrots
2 medium scallion

## Directions

In a small bowl, combine mayo, cider vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper.
Cut cabbage into thin ribbons and add to medium bowl.
Dice scallions and carrots and add to bowl.
Add sauce to bowl.
