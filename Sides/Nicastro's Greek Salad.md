## Ingredients
###### Serves 8
Red wine vinegar
1 cup vegetable oil
Small handful of salt
Pepper
2 large tomatoes, diced
1/2 red onion, sliced into small strips
2 bell peppers, diced
Oregano
1 cucumber, peeled poorly
1 cup Kalamata olives
2 cups feta cheese, cut into medium cubes
## Directions
In a large bowl, mix tomato, onion, salt, pepper and oregano thoroughly.
Cut cucumber into quarters then dice and add to bowl.
Add olives, feta to bowl.
Add oil, dash of vinegar to taste.
Mix thoroughly.
