## Ingredients
###### Serves 6
4 chicken thighs, bone + skin
1 onion, diced
2 carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, diced
1 tart apple, diced
6 cups water
1 tbsp curry powder
Salt
Pepper
2 cups rice, [[Rice|cooked]]
## Directions
Sear chicken over high heat, remove and set aside.
Fry onions in a large pot until brown.
Add carrots, celery, and apple and soften.
Add water, curry, salt, and pepper.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer for 45 min.
Remove chicken, discard skin and bones.
Stir in rice, then remove from heat.
