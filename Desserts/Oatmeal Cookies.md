## Ingredients
3/4 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
2 tbsp water
3/4 cup flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
3 cups rolled oats
Add-ins (optional; raisins, cranberries, choc. chips...)
## Directions
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Cream butter, sugars, eggs, water on medium speed until fluffy.
Combine flour, soda, and cinnamon in separate mixing bowl.
Add dry ingredients to butter mix.
Stir in oats and add-ins.
Spoon tbsp sized balls onto parchment.
Bake for 12-15 minutes.

Press flat for crispy cookies, leave as balls for chewy.
