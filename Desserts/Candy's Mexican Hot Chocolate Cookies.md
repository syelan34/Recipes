## Ingredients
###### Makes 12-16 cookies
1 stick butter (melted)
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup All Purpose flour
1/4 cup natural cocoa powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup chocolate chips + some extra for later
1 cup mini marshmallows + some extra for later
1 tbsp cinnamon powder
1 tsp cayenne powder

## Directions
Cut the butter into chunks, [[Browned Butter|brown the butter]], transfer to dish and allow to cool completely.
Mix all dry ingredients (minus brown sugar) in a bowl.
Cream butter with brown sugar until fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla extract, continue to beat until mixed.
Add dry ingredients to wet, mix continuously.
Stir in chocolate chips and mini marshmallows.
Wrap batter in saran wrap, let cool in fridge overnight.
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Divide batter into balls, place on parchment lined baking sheets.
Bake 12-15 minutes.
Press leftover chocolate chips and marshmallows into top of cookies while still malleable.
Transfer to cooling rack.
